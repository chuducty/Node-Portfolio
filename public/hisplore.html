A Brief Introduction

Designed by TREX inc. HISPLORE is a website designed to help educate students from grade 7 and up. The website aims to teach students about historical Australian people from as early as the 1900’s. This is achieved through the use of an interactive map and timeline combination, chosen for its intuitiveness and simplicity. The map is used to display markers, each representing a single person. When clicked these markers generate a floating div containing information on that person in the form of newspaper excerpts from Trove, links to these articles are also provided as is a link to the person's relevant wikipedia page.

Main Features:

  • Visual designs that are aesthetically pleasing and colour blind compatible.
  • Interactive third party map and timeline api’s.
  • The ability to find historical people throughout the and view articles from Trove on them.
